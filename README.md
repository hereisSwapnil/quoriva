## ✨ Key Features

- **🔍 Universal Scholarly Search** – Query over 130 million papers across physical sciences, biomedicine, and general academia in one unified interface.
- **🔗 Direct URL Resolution** – Paste a paper URL from any major repository, and Quoriva will instantly resolve the metadata and fetch the abstract.
- **🧠 Expert Explainer AI** – Powered by advanced LLMs (GPT-4o), Quoriva breaks down complex methodologies, findings, and implications into seven readable sections.
- **🌿 Evidence-First Approach** – Every explanation is anchored in original sources with direct links back to the primary paper for verification.
- **📱 Responsive & Editorial UI** – A premium, mobile-first design system utilizing **Fraunces** and **Cabin** typography for a calm, academic reading experience.

## 🏗️ The Intelligent Pipeline

Quoriva isn't just a wrapper; it's a sophisticated data-aggregation engine:

1.  **Parallel Multi-Source Search**: Simultaneous queries to Semantic Scholar, PubMed, and arXiv ensure maximum coverage.
2.  **Deduplication & Ranking**: Results are deduplicated by title and ranked by citation count to prioritize high-impact research.
3.  **Pro-Briefing Synthesis**: Our "Senior Intelligence Analyst" prompt (GPT-4o) extracts data points, methodology rigor, and strategic implications that most AI tools miss.

## 🛠️ Performance & Tech Stack

Built with a focus on speed, clarity, and reliability:

- **Framework**: [Next.js 15 (App Router)](https://nextjs.org/) for optimized server-side rendering and routing.
- **Logic**: [React 19](https://react.dev/) + [TypeScript](https://www.typescriptlang.org/) for a robust, type-safe frontend.
- **Styling**: [Tailwind CSS v4](https://tailwindcss.com/) for high-performance, modern CSS.
- **Persistence**: [Redis](https://upstash.com/) for global caching of research explanations.
